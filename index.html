Folklore CD


Taylor Swift's eighth studio album, folklore, was released on the 24th of July 2020. This CD includes a lyric book and CD with all 16 tracks, and a bonus track titled, "the lakes."









Evermore CD

Evermore is Taylor Swift's ninth studio album. Released on December 11, 2020, less than 5 months after folklore. This CD has all 15 tracks along with 2 bonus tracks, titled "right where you left me" and "it's time to go."
